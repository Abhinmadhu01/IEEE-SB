IEEE KERALA GRADUATE STUDENT COMMUNITY TRAVANCORE HUB INAUGURATION

11 September 2024

The IEEE Kerala Graduate Student Community's Travancore Hub Inauguration took place on 11th September 2024 at the Kerala University of Digital Science Innovation and Technology. The event, organized by the IEEE Kerala Section, featured speeches from key leaders. Prof. Muhammed Kasim S, IEEE Kerala Section Chair, reflected on IEEEâ€™s journey in Kerala, discussing the formation of student branches, the upcoming Region 10 split in 2028, and the role of leadership teams. Prof. Nandan S highlighted initiatives aimed at improving student engagement and developing leadership skills. Prof. Alex P James discussed collaborative efforts between DUK and IEEE, such as partnerships with prestigious universities and the development of over 60 products in three years. The event also emphasized the importance of elections in IEEE, with a video message from Sameer, a candidate for the 2024-26 elections. The inauguration ended with a vote of thanks, marking a key moment for student collaboration within the IEEE community
